Social Computing Index covers institutions and scholars who have engaged in social computing based on representative social computing journals including IEEE Transactions on Computational Social Systems (TCSS), ACM Transactions on Social Computing (TSC) and Elsevier Social Networks (SocNets).


The code for the social computing index site is adapted from the CSrankings: http://csrankings.org. All code and data of CSrankings is available here: https://github.com/emeryberger/CSRankings.
